Perform a BLE scan.

We scan for BLE devices that are advertizing.

Parameters
[in]durationThe duration that the scan is to run for measured in seconds.
[in]scanTypeThe type of scanning requested. The choices are BLE_SCA_TYPE_PASSIVE and BLE_SCAN_TYPE_ACTIVE. The distinction between them is whether or not the advertizer has a scan response requested from it.
